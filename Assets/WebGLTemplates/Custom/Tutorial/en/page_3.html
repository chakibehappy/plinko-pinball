Multipliers Prize Table: There are three different sets of Multiplier Prize Tables, with one randomly selected at the start of each game round.

When a ball lands in a multiplier compartment, the corresponding multiplier is applied to the individual ball's bet amount to determine the prize payout. The positions of the yellow-framed multipliers may also be shuffled at the beginning of each round.
