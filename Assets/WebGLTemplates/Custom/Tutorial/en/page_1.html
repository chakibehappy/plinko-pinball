Plinko Pinball

Gameplay Overview

The game screen is divided into two parts, the Pinball Arena and the Plinko zone.

Launch your ball into the alien-themed Pinball Arena, where hitting the glowing bumpers can make it bounce and potentially unlock massive multipliers at the bottom. Then, watch as your ball drops into the Plinko zone, where each multiplier compartment holds a unique multiplier—potentially reaching a mega 170x payout!
