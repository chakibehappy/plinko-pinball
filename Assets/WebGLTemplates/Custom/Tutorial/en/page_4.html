Plinko Pinball

Game Elements

Three sets of different Multipliers Prize Tables appear at random in each round of game play.

Free Ball Hole — A ball entering this hole grants an extra ball for the current round. Maximum of one free ball can be awarded in each round.

Bumpers — Deflect the ball upon contact, altering its trajectory.

Rocket Bumpers — Deflect the ball on contact, changing its direction. Hitting this bumper activates a bonus multiplier.
