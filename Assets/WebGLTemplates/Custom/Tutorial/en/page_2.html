Plinko Pinball

Bet Mechanism

  • Choose Your Bet: Determine your bet amount per ball for each game round.
  • Total Bet Calculation: Your bet amount is multiplied by 5 as each game round consists of 5 balls, which are launched separately.
  • Multipliers: Each ball can land on a different multiplier, bringing unexpected surprises!
  • Final Prize: The total prize from all 5 balls is accumulated and awarded at the end of the game round.
