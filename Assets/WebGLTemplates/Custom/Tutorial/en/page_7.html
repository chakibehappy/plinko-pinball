Plinko Pinball

How to Play

1. Adjust your bet amount using the - or + button on the betting panel.

Min Bet: 1DR 500

Max Bet: IDR 10M
2. Your total bet is multiplied by 5, as each round consists of 5 balls launched separately.
3. Tap "Start Round" to confirm your bet and begin game play.
4. Tap the "Launch Ball" button to release one ball at a time.
5. You can launch up to five balls in succession without waiting for the previous ball to complete its path.
